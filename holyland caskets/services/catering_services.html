Funeral Catering, unlike other types of catering services, requires more thoughtfulness on the part of the caterer. The menu, choices of food as well as the table setting should be appropriate for the type of funeral being held, taking into consideration, the race or religion of the deceased and his family as well as overall funeral set ups & aesthetics. For example, what’s right for a Catholic funeral may not be appropriate for a Taoist funeral wake and so forth. Hence, having your funeral director co-ordinate the catering of your food is often the safest choice; we will know exactly what’s appropriate and what’s not.

Difference Between funeral and other types of caterers

Funeral Catering needs to be simple yet dignified and above all affordable. Hence caterers, inexperienced in handling Funeral Catering may sometimes get it wrong.
